About Potentials Radar

Potentials Radar is the ultimate companion app for FC Career Mode players. With the latest FC 26 database, smart analysis tools, and unique features, it helps you scout, compare, and build your dream team with ease.

Key Features

  • Advanced player search and filters
  • PlayStyles+ and role-based recommendations
  • Head-to-head player comparison
  • Contract expiring alerts
  • AI-powered career team recommendation bot (coming soon)
